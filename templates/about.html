ThreadSwap was created to give people a place to swap their clothes, to reduce one-time-use-buying of clothes, or "Fast Fashion". Instead of buying an item of clothing, wearing it once and hiding it away from the world for eternity, you can stick it up on here and exchange it for something else.

How It Works

Step 1: Get an account with us

Step 2: Find an item you like

Step 3:Request a swap

Step 4:The owner of the item will receive a notification, and choose from your items

Step 5: You'll get a request, asking for your confirmation

Step 6:Happy with the choice? Confirm it and you'll get each others contact details to arrange pick up
